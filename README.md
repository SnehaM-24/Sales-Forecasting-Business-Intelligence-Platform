# Sales Forecasting & Business Intelligence Platform

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover business insights and forecast future sales trends using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective is to help decision-makers understand sales performance, identify profitable products and regions, and improve inventory planning through forecasting.

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large volumes of transactional data. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify profitable products, monitor sales performance, optimize inventory, and plan future growth.

This project provides a complete analytics solution that combines data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, SQL-based business reporting, forecasting, and interactive dashboards.
